Shaykh Ibn Bāz رحمه الله said:

“If a person does not attend lessons of knowledge, nor listen to sermons, nor pay attention to what is conveyed from the people of knowledge, then his heedlessness increases. And his heart may even harden until it becomes sealed and stamped, and he becomes among the heedless.”

📚 Majmū‘ al-Fatāwā (12/324)

> Tr: Umm ʿAbdirraḥmān as-Sūdāniyyah
