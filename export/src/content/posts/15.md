**Al-Ḥāfiẓ al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī رحمه الله said:**

“It befits the one whose time has been expanded for him, whose body Allāh the Exalted has kept sound,

and whom He has made to love rising above the ranks of the ignorant,

and placed in his heart the determination to gain understanding in the religion—

that he should seize the opportunity to hasten toward that,

out of fear that something may occur which will cut him off from it.”
