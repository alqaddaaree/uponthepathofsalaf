Today, people boast about their knowledge and pass it around without any concern for acting upon it. They strive for a large following and fame, forgetting that what they have learned will be a burden and a source of harm against them on the Day of Judgment...

Al-Marroudhi, may Allah have mercy on him, said: “Ahmad told me: ‘I never wrote down a hadith from the Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, without acting upon it. For example, I came across a hadith that the Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, had cupping (Hijama) done and gave Abu Tayyiba, the cupper, a dinar. So I had cupping done and gave the cupper a dinar.’”

- | Al-Jami’ by Al-Khatib (1/1480) | -

- Amr ibn Qays, may Allah have mercy on him, said: “If you hear something good, act upon it, and you will be among its people, even if only once.”

- | Az-Zuhd by Imam Ahmad ibn Hanbal (223) | -
