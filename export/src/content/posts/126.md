*"ذَلِكَ فَضْلُ اللَّهِ يُؤْتِيهِ مَنْ يَشَاءُ وَاللَّهُ ذُو الْفَضْلِ الْعَظِيمِ"

'That is the Grace of Allâh, which He bestows on whom He wills. And Allâh is the Owner of Mighty Grace.' [al-Jumuʿah: 4]*

*(hilāli & khān translation)*

**Tarbiyah-related benefits:**

In Allāh’s statement: *'That is the Grace of Allâh, which He bestows on whom He wills. And Allâh is the Owner of Mighty Grace.'* we understand that whoever receives a portion of the religion of Islām has indeed received a great favour. The blessing of Allāh has become immense upon them. How urgently they need, then, to be grateful for this blessing, to ask Allāh for its continuance, and to remain steadfast upon it until death. Dying upon it completes and perfects the blessing!

> Reference: Laṭā’if al-Ma‘ārif by Ibn Rajab (p. 85).

–

Tr: Umm ʿAbdirraḥmān as-Sūdāniyyah
