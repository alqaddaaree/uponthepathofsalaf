For the grief-stricken heart

For the heart that feels darkened with sorrow

For the heart that feels behind on blessings

For the heart that is lonely whilst he’s in a gathering of people

For the heart that is waiting for relief

This is the call to empty your heart of everything except the Most Merciful. Empty it of all worldly distractions and attach it to Allāh سبحانه وتعالى.

Allāh says in a ḥadīth qudsi:

*“O son of Ādam, busy yourself with My worship, and I will fill your heart with richness and contentment and fill your hands with provision; but if you do not do that, then I will fill your heart with poverty and busy your hands with distractions and I will not alleviate your poverty.”

[Sunan al-Tirmidhi‌ 2466]*

And do not let this worldly life distract you from what you are truly chasing.

Be with the companionship of the creation in body, and let your heart roam around your Beloved, let your soul reside in a higher abode.

This life was never meant to be a Jannah.

يقول أهل الجنة إذا دخلوها: {الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَذْهَبَ عَنَّا ٱلْحَزَن}

The people of Jannah when they enter it, they will say:

“All the praises and thanks be to Allāh Who has removed from us (all) grief and sorrow.”

*[Sūrah Fāṭir:34]

*Await the reward of your Rabb. Indeed, Allāh does not fail in His promise.

{إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُخْلِفُ ٱلْمِيعَادَ}

*[Sūrah Āl ʿImrān:9]

*
