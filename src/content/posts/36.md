Imām al-Saʿdī رحمه الله says in his tafsīr of verse 98 of Sūrat al-Ḥijr:

“So you, O Muḥammad, ‘exalt with praise of your Lord and be among those who prostrate’ — meaning: increase in the remembrance of Allāh, in glorifying Him, praising Him, and in prayer. For indeed, that expands and opens the chest and helps you in your affairs.”
