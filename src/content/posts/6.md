If I am correct, then the credit is due to Allah. And if I am mistaken, then it is from myself and from Shaytan, for I am not infallible. And if I ever spread something that calls to other than the haqq or ṣawab, then I ask for sincere advice, and I pray for whoever advises and corrects me. May Allah reward you with goodness.
