**Shaykh al-Albani رحمه اللّٰه said:**

"The truth is heavy. So, do not make it heavier with your bad manners. I used to believe that the (main) problem in the Ummah is 'aqidah, but it became clear to me that it is 'aqidah and manners."

**Source: Silsila al-Huda wa al-Nur, Tape 900

**

> Tr: Umm ʿAbdirraḥmān as-Sūdāniyyah
