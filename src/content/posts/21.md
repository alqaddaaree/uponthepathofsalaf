**السلام عليكم،

What is the ruling on using the heart symbol (❤)?**

*In the Name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate.*

There is a fatwa by the Permanent Committee (24/92), headed by Shaykh Ibn Bāz رحمه الله and Shaykh Ṣāliḥ al-Fawzān حفظه الله, in response to a question that was presented to them along with other issues. The fatwa stated:

That it involves imitation of the people of immorality, who take such symbols as an indication of their love and forbidden passion for others, becoming absorbed in it without regard for the ruling of the pure Sharīʿah concerning it. End quote (paraphrased).

After the ruling has been established that it is imitation of the people of immorality, the ḥadīth of the Prophet ﷺ is well known:

*“Whoever imitates a people is one of them.”*

My advice to myself and to everyone who reads this message is to refer, in our doubts and issues, back to the firmly grounded scholars, so that we may be upon light and insight in the matters of our religion. For Allah the Mighty and Majestic says:

> “And when there comes to them a matter of security or fear, they spread it. But if they had referred it to the Messenger and to those in authority among them, then those who are able to draw correct conclusions from it would have known it.”

(Qur’an 4:83)

Umm ʿAbdillāh, daughter of Shaykh Muqbil رحمه الله

The tenth night of the month of Muḥarram, 1437 AH.
