**The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:**

> إنَّ الرِّفْقَ لا يَكونُ في شَيءٍ إلَّا زانَهُ، ولا يُنْزَعُ مِن شَيءٍ إلَّا شانَهُ.

“Gentleness is not present in anything except that it beautifies it, and it is not removed from anything except that it disgraces it.”

Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim (2594)
