وَلَقَدْ نَعْلَمُ أَنَّكَ يَضِيقُ صَدْرُكَ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ ﴿٩٧﴾

"And We already know that your chest [I.e heart] is constricted by what they say"

فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَكُنْ مِنَ السَّاجِدِينَ ﴿٩٨﴾

"So exalt [Allah] with praise of your Lord, and be of those who prostrate [to Him]"

Al Hijr: 97-98
