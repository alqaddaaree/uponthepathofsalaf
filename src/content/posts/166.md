**Women of the Salaf || نِسَاءُ السَّلَف**

> 🌸 Shaykha Umm ʿAbdillāh mentioned her father’s (Shaykh Muqbil) advice to Women:

It is upon the women to study the biography of the Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ and the biographies of the female companions who lived during the Prophet's time, such as Umm Sulaym and other virtuous women of the Companions.

Follow their example if you hope to attain Allāh's pleasure and the Hereafter.

[مقدمة والدي لـ نصيحتي للنساء].
