Anas ibn Mālik رضي الله عنه reported that the Messenger of Allah ﷺ said:

> “Indeed, the Ḥūr al-ʿĪn will sing in Paradise, saying: ‘We are the beautiful Ḥūr, preserved for noble husbands.’”
