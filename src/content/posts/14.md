The Messenger of Allah ﷺ slept on a mat. When he got up, it had left an impression on his side. We said: “O Messenger of Allah, shall we make a mattress for you?”

He said: “What is there for me with the world? I am in this world only like a traveler who takes shelter under a tree for a while, then moves on and leaves it.”

**

— Sahih al-Tirmidhi

**

> Tr: Umm ʿAbdirraḥmān as-Sūdāniyyah
