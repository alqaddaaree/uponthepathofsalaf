ʿIkrimah – may Allāh have mercy upon him – said:

“**Everyone gets happy and gets sad, but you should turn your happiness into gratitude [to Allāh] and your sadness into patience (ṣabr).**”

[Tafsīr Ibn Kathīr, Al-Ḥadīd: 23]
